Introduction to the Hiroshima-Nagasaki attacks: The United States used Nuclearbombs on the Japanese cities of Hiroshma and Nagasaki on 6 th and 9 th August 1945 respectively. It was the 1st use of nuclear weapons in wartime and till date remains the only one.
